Bio

Kelseigh was born on September 25, 1994 at Norton Hospital in Louisville, Kentucky. A short while after her birth, she was rushed over to Kosair Children's Hospital for her first heart surgery.

Kelseigh was diagnosed with Turner Syndrome before she was even born. Turner Syndrome is a chromosomal disorder that affects one in every 2,000 living people who were assigned female at birth. This is due to a partially or completely missing X chromosome.

Some things that come with having Turner Syndrome include short stature, infertility, and heart and kidney problems.
